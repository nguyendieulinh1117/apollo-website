Apollo HCMC - Group Learning Experience Manager
FULL TIME
JOB DESCRIPTION

Apollo Junior is currently looking for an experienced manager to take control of it’s 2 centers in Ho Chi Minh City with a start date in July / August 2021. The Group Learning Experience Manager will manage the overall learning experience of these centers.

We have more than 25 years of experience teaching English in Vietnam and currently operate more than 50 training centres nationwide. Apollo Junior offers a range of young learner classes, teaching 3 to 17 year olds with the goal of creating a rewarding and high quality learning environment.

At Apollo you can expect:
  • A commitment to ongoing professional development and high-quality teaching standards
  • A wide range of resources and modern facilities
  • A variety of courses and learners
  • Academic and personal support from a dedicated team
  • Opportunities for career development
At Apollo you can expect:

The role of the Group Learning Experience Manager is to plan, implement, and continuously improve the delivery of a high quality and efficient EFL teaching program in line with the strategic goals of both the​ ​centers and the organisation. This also requires working closely with 2 Centre Managers (CM) to understand and deliver the business needs of all concerned. The GLXM must also be an outstanding representative of Apollo English inside and outside of work, by upholding the company values and guiding principles at all times.

TIME ALLOCATION: GLXMs are expected to spend a minimum of 40 hours on site each working week (split between the 2 centers.

Essential requirements for all teaching positions:
  • CELTA / Trinity TESOL / IHC or equivalent / DELTA, MA Tesol or DipTesol preferred
  • At least 3 years teaching experience (management experience preferred).
  • Bachelor’s Degree or higher
  • Native level English speaker (USA, UK, Australia and Canada preferred)
  • Clean criminal record check (with validity within 6 months)
  • Adaptability, flexibility, and cultural sensitivity
  • 6 months’ validity on passport
APPLICATION INFOMATION
Remuneration:

Apollo offers a highly competitive pay which is based on qualifications and experience.

Application Information:

Your application should include a cover letter, your updated CV in English and scanned copies of your degree(s) and Teaching Certificates. Candidates who have already obtained a current valid Police Check preferred. Submission of original documents will be required later for Work Permit application, photocopies will not be accepted.

*Only short-listed candidates will be contacted.
