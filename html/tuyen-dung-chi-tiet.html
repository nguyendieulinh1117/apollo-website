MARKETING EXECUTIVE
Job Description
  • Plan and manage Marketing projects from coordinating for implementation, controlling delivery of tasks, to project budget managing and analyzing the results and the effectiveness of the projects
  • Proactively monitor the assigned Center’s performance in terms of Sales, inquiries and presentation of the Center (brand quality is met at the highest)
  • Manage key partners to assure smooth implementation of partnerships and a good relationship
  • Support in analyzing competitors and frequently update the competitive landscape in order to help Apollo keep up a stable and good speed against competitors.
  • Assist in looking out for development and growth opportunities of Apollo in all aspects: increase brand exposure/awareness, increase database, etc…
  • Perform other duties designated by the Line Manager.
Job Requirements
  • Vietnamese national
  • Holder of Bachelor’s degree
  • Knowledgeable about marketing skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, both in English and Vietnamese
  • Be proactive and initiative
  • Problem solving and analytical skills
  • Business-oriented, result-driven, flexible with passion to learn
Job Location
  • Hải Phòng
  • Đà Nẵng
  • Hà Nội
